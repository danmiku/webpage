Dan Mikulincer's Homepage

I am a Ph.D. student at at the Weizmann insitutue, Faculty of Mathematics. My advisor is Ronen Eldan.
Before coming to Weizmann, I completed my B.Sc. at Ben-Gurion University (BGU).
I've spent the summer of 2019 at Microsoft Research AI, where I was hosted by Sébastien Bubeck.
I am also an Azrieli fellow.

My research interests broadly lie at the union of high-dimensional geometry, probability, statistics, information theory and their relation to data science and learning theory. I am particulary ineterested in normal approximations and dimension-free phenomena.
